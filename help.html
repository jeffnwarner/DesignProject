Help

What does having an account do?

Signing up for an account with Lucid You allows a users to a dream journal in which users can log their dreams in. It also allows the user to signup for the USER Forum, allowing their logs to be shown anonymously with other people to further research in Lucid Dreaming.

How do I signup for an account?

To signup a user needs to input a unique Email, with a unique 8 character long password!

You can go here to signup!

What is the user Forum?

The user forum is a place in which a user can see the dream logs of other people who has agreed to post their accounts. This allows other people and researchers to search for dream logs that could help people fall into Lucid Dreaming.

It provides a space in which people can compare their experiences with the app, and see which settings best suit them to go into Lucid Dreams!

How do I become a part of the online forum?

Initially, a person is not a part of the online forum!

To opt into the forum, a user needs to go to the settings page, and click on the subscribe button. This will post all of their dreams to the forum.
If a person decides to want to remove themselves from the forum, they would need to click on the unsubscribe button. This will remove all of their posts from the forum and prevent future records from appearing on the forum until they re-scubsribe.
